Our Activities Details

Visit Sweet Home

We had the privilege of visiting Sweet Home Sukkur and spending quality time with the children. We sincerely thank Mr. Shabir Memon, Assistant Director, for his support and collaboration on the HEAL Project, which made this initiative successful.

Blood Donation Camp

HEAL Organization successfully organized a Blood Donation Camp in collaboration with its partners. The initiative aimed to promote the spirit of saving lives and supporting those in need, encouraging the community to come together for a meaningful cause.

Iftar Dinner Dastarkhuwa

HEAL Organization successfully organized an Iftar Dastarkhuwan for the community in collaboration with VNG, LAS, and Youth Empowerment Affairs. This initiative aimed to share blessings, spread kindness, and bring people together during Ramadan—small efforts creating meaningful impact.

Youth Empowerment Session

HEAL Organization successfully organized a Youth Empowerment Session in collaboration with its partners. The initiative aimed to provide young people with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in life, encouraging them to become active contributors to their communities.

Cancer Awareness

HEAL Organization successfully organized a Cancer Awareness campaign in collaboration with its partners. The initiative aimed to educate the community about cancer prevention and early detection, promoting a healthier lifestyle.

Ramadan Nutrition Awareness Session

Team HEAL successfully organized the second Ramadan session on “Balancing Your Diet During Ramadan” in collaboration with Cure Link Health Care and VNG. The session was conducted by Dt. Ayesha Sajjad, focusing on the importance of maintaining a balanced and healthy diet while fasting. It provided valuable guidance to help individuals stay energized and make better nutritional choices throughout Ramadan.
